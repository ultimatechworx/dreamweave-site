Age Groups

Stories that grow with your child

Dreambean adapts story complexity, vocabulary, and illustrations based on your child's age group.

🌙
Ages 5 – 8

Explorer

Simple, warm, and magical stories told in the present tense with gentle vocabulary and bold, colorful illustrations.

  • Short sentences and simple words
  • Present tense narration
  • Bold, friendly illustrations
  • 60 – 80 words per chapter
⚡
Ages 9 – 12

Creator

Richer adventures with more complex plots, atmospheric illustrations, and expanded vocabulary to challenge growing readers.

  • Richer vocabulary and descriptive language
  • Past tense narrative style
  • Atmospheric, detailed illustrations
  • 100 – 140 words per chapter

Child Safety

Built safe from the ground up

Every layer of Dreambean is designed to keep young imaginations in a safe, positive space.

🛡️

AI Content Moderation

Every text input is screened before reaching the story engine. Inappropriate content is blocked instantly with a child-friendly message.

👨‍👩‍👧

Parent-Controlled Accounts

Accounts are created by parents or guardians. Age groups are protected by a math challenge gate so only adults can change them.

🔍

Story Safety Review

Every AI-generated story is reviewed by a second AI layer before delivery to ensure age-appropriate content and positive themes.

🚫

No Ads. Ever.

Dreambean contains zero advertisements. Children's attention and data are never sold to advertisers.

🔒

Minimal Data Collection

We collect only an email address for account management. Story content is AI-generated and never used to profile your child.

✅

COPPA Compliant

Dreambean is designed to comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act. Parent accounts ensure we never collect data directly from children.
